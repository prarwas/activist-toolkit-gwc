why is mental health awareness necessary ?

1 in 4 people (approximately 450 million globally) suffer from mental disorders, making it more common than physical diseases. signs of mental illness usually become present at a very young age (50% at age 14 and 75% at age 25). and most importantly, mental illness contributes to 90% of suicides.

source : clubhouse international.

stigma + discrimination prevents people from seeking aid

both can worsen the condition of a person. they are denied so many oppurtunities by society due to the stereotypes against mental disorders. social isolation, poor housing, unemployment, and poverty are all linked to mental ill health. - mental health foundation

sufficient isn’t care is given to mental health patients in unfortunate countries

"The lack of resources is also one of the most prominent challenges. The [OHCHR] report indicates that despite the impact of mental health conditions on individuals, families and communities, there is inadequate investment of both financial and human resources to mental health. For an example, the global annual spending on mental health is reported to be less than $2 per person and less than $0.25 per person in low-income countries" - American Psychological Association
