how to know if you’re discriminatory towards the mentally ill
  1. When your friend is having a difficult time with anxiety do you ask them how they’re doing and that they should seek professional help similar to how you'd ask if they broke a bone?
  2. Do you judge someone if they take an antidepressant but understand when someone takes insulin for diabetes?
  3. Do you not visit someone if they’re in the hospital for psychological problems but go right away if they're suffering with a fatal disease?

If you answered YES for any of these, then the problem starts with you.

You can tackle the stigma and discrimination within you by starting with this action list.

my action item list

click it to check it off !!

  • go get a mental health checkup because it requires as much attention as your physical health
